Room Types

Single Occupancy Room

This room is for one person, equipped with one single bed.

Family Room

This room is for two people, equipped with one double bed, so-called queen size bed.

Duplex Room

It's a two-level room. It consists of at least one bedroom on one floor and a living room or bedroom on the second floor. These types of rooms are commonly chosen by families with small children who go to bed much earlier, and thanks to two levels they are provided with more comfort and silence.
